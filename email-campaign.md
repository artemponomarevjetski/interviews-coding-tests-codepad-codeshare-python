## 26. Recruiter Quality / Fidelity Rules

High-quality recruiter/thread signals:

- Provides client name
- Provides compensation range
- Gives direct phone number
- Asks concrete screening questions
- Offers call or calendar
- Role aligns with AI/ML/Python/platform
- Mentions direct submission to client
- Works with high-value client or startup
- Has specific JD and timeline
- Replies quickly and asks for next-step materials

Low-quality signals:

- No client
- No rate
- vague role
- mass email
- unrelated skills
- asks for sensitive documents early
- low rate
- asks for references before interview
- sends only a job-board link with no relationship
- pushes financial/tax/wealth services instead of hiring

Score recruiters as:

```text
A — direct client / high comp / clear role / fast process
B — credible agency / clear JD / decent rate / responsive
C — vague or low signal, but potentially useful
D — low-rate spam, irrelevant, or asks for sensitive docs too early
```
